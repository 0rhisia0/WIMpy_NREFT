# WIMpy_NREFT


## NREFT form factors

These are listed in the following format:

$F_M^{(p,p)}$
$F_M^{(p,n)}$
$F_M^{(n,p)}$
$F_M^{(n,n)}$
$F_{\Sigma'}^{(p,p)}$
$F_{\Sigma'}^{(p,n)}$
$F_{\Sigma'}^{(n,p)}$
$F_{\Sigma'}^{(n,n)}$
$F_{\Sigma''}^{(p,p)}$
$F_{\Sigma''}^{(p,n)}$
$F_{\Sigma''}^{(n,p)}$
$F_{\Sigma''}^{(n,n)}$
$F_{\Delta}^{(p,p)}$
$F_{\Delta}^{(p,n)}$
$F_{\Delta}^{(n,p)}$
$F_{\Delta}^{(n,n)}$
$F_{\Phi''}^{(p,p)}$
$F_{\Phi''}^{(p,n)}$
$F_{\Phi''}^{(n,p)}$
$F_{\Phi''}^{(n,n)}$
$F_{M,\Phi''}^{(p,p)}$
$F_{M,\Phi''}^{(p,n)}$
$F_{M,\Phi''}^{(n,p)}$
$F_{M,\Phi''}^{(n,n)}$
$F_{\Sigma',\Delta}^{(p,p)}$
$F_{\Sigma',\Delta}^{(p,n)}$
$F_{\Sigma',\Delta}^{(n,p)}$
$F_{\Sigma',\Delta}^{(n,n)}$

### References

NREFT form factors for Xe, F and I are taken from [arXiv:1203.3542](https://arxiv.org/abs/1203.3542). The form factor for C is taken from [arXiv:1501.03729](https://arxiv.org/abs/1501.03729).
